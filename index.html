About Me

Who am i?

I am a interventionist and change coach who has personally experienced the joy of inner transformation. I believe that we all have a powerful, wise, and creative core that can unleash our potential. I partner with organizations and their leaders to help them embark on their own transformation journeys. I help them build a deep awareness of their beliefs, values, threats, and aspirations, and align them with their desired future. From this space of possibility and power, I guide them to take actions that lead to positive change.
